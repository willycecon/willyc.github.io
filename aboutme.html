I am a first-year Economics PhD student at Michigan State University.

I obtained my MA in Economics from the University of Chicago in August 2020 and BS in Economics and Mathematics from The Ohio State University in May 2019.

My research interest is in labor economics and the economics of inequality with a current focus on the migration effect of Sanctuary City Policies.
