Willy (Wei-Li) Chen

I am a Pre-Doctoral Research Associate under Professor Amy Ellen Schwartz at Syracuse University.

I obtained my MA in Economics from the University of Chicago in August 2020 and BS in Economics and Mathematics from The Ohio State University in May 2019.

My research interest is in public economics with a current focus on the migration effect of Sanctuary City Policies.
